What is PERA?

PERA (Personal Expenses Recording Assistant) is designed to help users take control of their financial life. It simplifies tracking income, monitoring expenses, and planning for future savings.

By giving you clear insights into your spending habits, PERA encourages smarter financial decisions and better money management.
